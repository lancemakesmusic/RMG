Biography

Built on perseverance and passion.

Luchiano Emilio is an artist from New Orleans whose musical journey began in third grade. Raised in New Orleans East by his mother, he honed his songwriting skills while studying strings in middle school. Despite bullying and financial challenges, music was his solace, with his mother being his biggest supporter. Luchiano’s unique sound blends raw emotion with insightful lyrics, earning him the nickname “Mobster” from older peers. His story of perseverance and passion, from humble beginnings to making his mark in the music industry, continues to inspire.
